Accessibility Options!

Tritanopia

Unable to tell the difference between blue and green, purple and red, and yellow and pink. It also makes colors look less bright.

Deuteranopia

This is when your M cones are missing. So, you can't perceive green light. You mostly see blues and golds. You may confuse some shades of red with some shades of green. You may also confuse yellows with bright shades of green.

Protanopia

A state in which the red cones are absent, leaving only the cones that absorb blue and green light.
